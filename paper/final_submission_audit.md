## Claim Hygiene

The manuscript now avoids claiming that TeleMin-RAG is a universally superior
classifier or that it dominates every dataset. The central claim is the
security-aware context-construction layer:

> TeleMin-RAG provides a competitive and auditable preprocessing layer that
> reduces context and poison exposure while preserving or improving F1 in several
> SOC triage settings.
